Mortgage and residential survey

A mortgage is often required when purchase a property. Also real estate market need a mortgage survey for inspect a property. For the property the physical inspect is needed but not enough. Then mortgage survey is play important role. It verifies the boundary of the property enchroachments and other structures that exist on it. Mortgage survey is also needed when looking for a mortgage loan.

Boundary survey

In simple words, boundary survey means boundaries of a property. The boundary survey is needed when before the property is buying, dividing, improving or building on land. It focuses largely on defining the corners of parcel of land. In boundary survey fences are mostly used as way to know where your property is however, they are rarely built directly on the property lines. After boundary survey is complete, the CAD drawing is needed for better measurement accuracy.

ALTA/NSPS/ACSM Title Survey

ALTA/NSPS/ACSM Title Survey are detailed, also including important information about property such as real property, boundaries, property line, enchroachments, easements, liens and land ownership data. This survey is to provide consistency and uniformity to the title industry. ALTA surveys show land ownership, easements, right of way, hidden assets, parcel boundaries and unrecorded liens. ALTA survey is needed before buying or financing real property when there is a need for deeply detailed information such as location of sewer line, fences, etc.

Topographic survey

Topographic survey also known as contours. Topographic survey measure and show all components which natural and human made within an area of surveys land. Also topographic survey is a need for to give specification about the objects which natural and humans made survey land. Natural features included trees and ground contours and human features such as boundaries, walkway, neibouring building, etc.

Subdivision plat

Subdivision plat is a map drawn with specified scale to track division of land. These indicate lot and block designation on the plat. Plat is show the boundaries of easements, alley ways and street.

Tree Service

Tree survey is an important survey which is performed on private or public landscape. Tree survey included all information about tree. Such as specific name of tree, age of tree, overall health of tree, etc. The all information is play the important role for property owner may then decide what to do with tree.
